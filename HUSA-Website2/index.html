Howard University's Student Association is the primary student advocacy group, representing the entire HU student population, this entity is the legislative body that reflects the needs of students. HUSA serves as the direct liason and communicative voice between students and administration. HUSA was established in 1961.




















HUSA 2018 Candidates





Ubuntu Advocacy

We define our identity based on who we are as students. HUSA works to rally all student voices and concerns to inform the administration for better university policies and culture.




Programming to enhance the Howard experience

We use our allocated budget to create programs, policies, and initiatives that empower the mind, body, and soul of the student body.





Community Development

We work with student and community organizations to develop the Howard community at large by building advocates to work on and off campus.




#Wakandaforever

We strive to be the examples of Black Excellence that we want to see. It is one thing to say that Howard University is the mecca, but it is another to live as the mecca. HUSA recognizes the vibranium that exists in every student. Therefore, we empower our peers to be the community that is never conquered, the community that always grows, and the community that never freezes.
